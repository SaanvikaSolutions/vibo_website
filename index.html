Welcome To Vibo Aesthetics

At Vibo Aesthetics, we specialize in enhancing beauty and confidence through precision-driven, personalized care. Led by the highly acclaimed Dr. Viraja Bobburi, M.S., M.Ch., a board-certified plastic, reconstructive, and cosmetic surgeon, our clinic is dedicated to delivering world-class aesthetic treatments in the heart of Vijayawada. With a track record of over 6000 successful procedures and a commitment to innovative, patient-centric solutions, we strive to craft natural, beautiful results tailored to each individual’s needs.

Our range of services encompasses advanced hair, skin, face, and body treatments, all designed to help you look and feel your best. Whether you're seeking a subtle enhancement or a transformative procedure, Vibo Aesthetics is your trusted partner in beauty and wellness.

Our Services

Hair Treatments

At Vibo Aesthetics, we offer advanced hair treatments to address various concerns. Our Hair Transplantation services restore your natural hairline using techniques like Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). We also provide effective solutions for hair fall, including PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma), GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate), and mesotherapy, which nourish and stimulate hair follicles. For those looking to reduce unwanted hair, our state-of-the-art laser hair reduction techniques ensure long-lasting results.

Skin Treatments

Our comprehensive skin treatments cater to a wide range of conditions. We effectively manage acne with personalized plans involving topical treatments and advanced procedures. For issues like pigmentation, birthmarks, and stretch marks, we utilize laser treatments and chemical peels to enhance skin tone and texture. Our services also include tattoo removal, targeting unwanted ink with precision lasers. Additionally, we address scars (hypertrophic and keloid), warts, skin tags, moles, psoriasis, and vitiligowith effective, minimally invasive procedures.

Face Treatments

Enhance your facial features with our diverse range of treatments. Our permanent makeup services offer long-lasting beauty enhancements for eyebrows, lips, cheeks, and scalp. Experience the rejuvenating effects of our Hydrafacial, which deeply cleanses and hydrates your skin. We also provide chemical peels to improve skin clarity and texture. For more pronounced changes, our surgical options include blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery), rhinoplasty (nose reshaping), and options for lip augmentation or reduction. Additional offerings include ear piercing, ear lobe repair, dimple creation, and chin implants for a balanced, harmonious appearance.
